Welcome to Learn N Play DayCare

We are the premier early childhood learning center in Southwest Houston, TX. Our affordable and unique learning approach gives every child in the City of Houston an aooprtunity to thrive.

Our childcare center provides care for children ages 6 weeks-12 years old. Our Preschool Program is perfect for 2-3 years old and provides early learners with the essentials that will equip them for the next stage.

Our Pre-K program is designed for children 4-5 years old and provides learners with core academics and readiness skills that will help them transition into the school years.

Our school-aged program provides before and afterschool planned activities, enrichment activities , reading programs and homework assistance to keep the children engaged and ready to excel.
