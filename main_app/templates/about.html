About

Learn N Play DayCare is comitted to providing quality early childhood education at affordable rates. At Learn N Play DayCare , provide children of Houston, TX an opportunity to thrive. We cater to the developmental, physical, emotional, intellectual and social needs of the children in our care by providing a safe, healthy and warm learning environment for children ages 6 weeks-12 years old.

All of our Early Childhood Educators are screened with an extensive application and interview process that includes reference checks with previous employers and all eligible applicants must clear a State and FBI background check. Our Early Childhood Educators are First Aid/CPR Certified and receive on-going training to meet Texas Child Care Licensing Minimum Standards requirements.

At no additional cost, we have a Family Advocate that is available to connect families to local community organizations that may be able to assist families with social and emotional needs.
